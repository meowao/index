Game Features

游戏特色

SURVIVE THE ZOMBIE APOCALYPSE IN Infinite WAYS!

🔥 RALLY YOUR SQUAD (Act I) Lead survivors through open warzones! Team up with specialized allies like the Shotgun Bruiser, discover powerful skill combinations, and collect randomized loot to build your ultimate zombie-fighting force.

🔥 COMMAND THE BATTLEFIELD (Act II) Deploy strategic defenses on a hexagonal grid! Position Flame Troopers to incinerate hordes and Sniper Phantoms to secure the skies. Master class synergies to defeat massive mutant titans and explosive zombie waves.

🔥 FACE THE HORROR ALONE (Act III) Enter a terrifying first-person nightmare in an abandoned lab! With only your flashlight cutting through darkness, scavenge for weapon blueprints and use environmental traps to survive relentless ambushes from lethal Lickers.

⚡️ Subsequent updates: More battlefield modes More new gameplay will be launched soon! From tower defense to card battles, from racing escape to strategy sandbox, each update brings a new combat experience!
